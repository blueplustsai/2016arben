ASI-706 布樣圓切器 Circle Sample Cutter

快速容易
QUICK & EASY

布料放在切片軟墊上，壓下手鈕並旋轉超過90度，即可切取正確的100平方公分布樣，用以秤量布重。
Place fabrics (avoid wrinkled) onto the cutting mat, press down hand wheel, rotate over 90 degrees under pressure, and you've get an accurate 100 cm2 sample for weighing.

製作迅速
PRODUCTIVITY

一次可裁切厚度15mm(裁刀全幅)，省時省功，是鋸齒剪刀百倍速度，大幅提高人員生產力。
Simply stack fabrics and cut. Cutting thickness is up to 15mm at full length of blade.
Productivity is hundred times of Pinking Shears.

精密加工
PRECISE MACHINEING

圓切器是由精密車製的鋁合金圓盤，與鋼製刀片組成，三個圓盤精密配合，並經特殊處理，操作平滑順暢，長期使用絕不磨損或卡死，鋁盤底部接觸面的溝槽設計，可確實壓穩布樣，使切取樣品邊緣完美。

At the bottom of disks, grooves design keeps fabrics firm, and samples cut off with clean and neat edges.

ASI-707 電子布重秤 Electronic Cloth Balance

大容量與精密感量
LARGE CAPACITY & PRECISE RESOLUTION

最大實際秤重量為150公克(5.29英兩)，換算最大布重達15,000公克/每平方米(442英兩/每平方碼，或20,900公克/每碼長-60英吋幅寬)，可秤任何類型布料，甚至地毯，感量為0.01公克(0.0005英兩)，換算布重誤差為1公克/每平方米(0.04英兩/每平方碼)，精確可靠。
Maximum actual weight capacity is 150 grams(5.29 oz). The scale is capable for all kinds of fabrics of up to 15,000 grams per square meter (442 Oz/yd2, or 20,900 g/yd-60”breadth), even carpet. The resolution is 0.01 gram (0.0005 oz), comes to fabrics of 1 g/m2 (0.04 Oz/yd2) precise tolerance.

多種計算 自動換算
MULTI-MEASUREMENT & AUTOMATIC CALCULATION

切取布樣100平方公分放在秤盤上，立即得知"實際重量"與"每平方米公克"，輸入幅寬，可同時顯示"全幅寬每碼長公克"，亦可切換為"每平方碼英兩(盎司)"(牛仔布常用)，任何幅寬(英吋)皆可按鍵輸入，自動換算，完全不需再使用計算機，多種計量方式適合各種需求。
Cut 100 cm2 fabric sample and put it on Scale Pan, you can get “Actual weight” and “Grams per square meter” (Actkual weight x 100, 1 m2 =10,000 cm2 = 100 x 100 cm2). Just key-in the fabric’s breadth, the Balance will simultaneously show “full-breadth grams per yard”. It also can be changed to “Ounces per square yard” (frequently used with JEAN). Any breadth (inches) can be key-in and automatically calculated. Calculator is no longer needed. Multi-measurement meets all demands.
